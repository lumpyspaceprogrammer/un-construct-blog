The 2-Dollar Dopamine Trap

It starts with an ad. Then a coupon. Then a "limited time" countdown that triggers a primal panic in the back of your brain. Before you know it, you've spent $40 on things you didn't know existed ten minutes ago.

This is the dopamine trap. It's designed to bypass your logic and hit the reward centers of your brain directly. The cost isn't just the money; it's the erosion of your intention.

When we buy garbage, we are telling the algorithm to keep making it. We are funding the destruction of quality.
